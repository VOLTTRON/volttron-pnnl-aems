Advanced Energy Management System

About This Deployment

This AEMS instance is deployed at the Pacific Northwest National Laboratory (PNNL) Research Operations Building (ROB). The system monitors and optimizes energy usage across the facility.

Building Information

  • Campus: PNNL
  • Building: Research Operations Building (ROB)
  • Location: Richland, WA
  • Time Zone: America/Los_Angeles

System Overview

The AEMS platform at ROB provides comprehensive building automation and energy management capabilities:

  • HVAC Control: Automated temperature and ventilation management
  • Energy Monitoring: Real-time tracking of power consumption
  • Demand Response: Dynamic load management and peak shaving
  • Analytics Dashboard: Detailed insights into building performance
  • Fault Detection: Automated alerts for system anomalies

Getting Started

Navigate through the AEMS interface to:

  • View real-time system status and sensor data
  • Configure occupancy schedules and setpoints
  • Review historical energy usage and trends
  • Generate reports and export data
  • Manage user access and permissions
